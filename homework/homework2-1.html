1. End with your "<" and "/"

1.1. Try Self help

Book: "HTML & CSS: Design and Build Websites"

by: Jon Ducket
Publisher: Wiley
Difficulty level: beginner 😀
Release Date: November 2011
ISBN: 9781118008188

At a glance, the book appears easy to follow along. A picture speaks of a thousand words type of material. Reader reviews elsewhere have mixed feelings. First, the publishing date November 2011 (c'mon! it's basically 2012) makes it outdated. Second, humans learn differently; thus, the book doesn't make everybody happy. Is it then a good choice for a beginner? I think so...
